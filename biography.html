Biography

Liang Xu received the B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Automation from Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China in 2011 and 2013, respectively. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. His research interests are in the areas of control, communication and information theory
